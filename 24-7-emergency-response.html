What Is 24/7 Emergency Response?

24/7 Emergency Response is our commitment to being available whenever your plumbing needs urgent attention. Unlike many local plumbers who close at 5 PM and take weekends off, Richardson Plumber maintains a dedicated on-call team that monitors emergency calls every hour of every day. When you call, you reach a live dispatcher—not a voicemail system or automated menu—who assesses your situation, prioritizes your need, and dispatches the nearest available licensed technician directly to your address.

This service is ideal for homeowners and property managers who cannot afford downtime when water damage threatens their property. Whether it is a burst pipe flooding your basement, an overflowing toilet creating a sanitation hazard, a main line clog backing up sewage, or a failed water heater leaving your family without hot water, our emergency response team is equipped to diagnose the problem on-site and, in most cases, complete a permanent repair the same day—minimizing disruption, limiting secondary damage, and restoring normalcy quickly.

The core problem we solve is the stress and damage that occur when plumbing fails outside regular business hours. Water damage spreads rapidly, can compromise structural integrity, promote mold growth, and create health hazards. By providing immediate, professional response and same-day repairs, we help you stop the crisis before it becomes a catastrophe. Our licensed technicians arrive fully equipped, diagnose the root cause (not just the symptom), and deliver code-compliant repairs that last—so you avoid repeat emergencies and hidden problems left behind walls or under floors.
